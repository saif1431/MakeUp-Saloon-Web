Hannah Raasch

Professional make-up artist

My name is Hannah Raasch and I am a trained beautician and make-up artist from Kassel. My passion is bringing together aesthetic make-up looks and craftsmanship, cosmetic expertise.

About me

My professional career as a beautician started in Leipzig when I began my three-year dual training at IBB Dresden and with Germany's best beautician - Ilka Lucas. The focus of the completed training was on professional skin analysis and advice, conceptual cosmetic applications, also in the area of ​​instrumental cosmetics, as well as creating make-up looks for events such as the Semper Opera Ball and other occasions.

I have always been fascinated by the special effect of well-groomed, radiant skin and natural make-up and the emotions that this can trigger in people. By becoming self-employed, I fulfilled my dream of turning my passion into a career in order to experience these emotions with my customers every day. The craft of cosmetics, as well as the precise fine work involved in creating make-up, are of the highest importance to me. My approach is to underline the natural in order to aesthetically highlight different facets.

For me, your skin is a matter of the heart. It is the cornerstone of well-being and self-confidence. That's why I take my time. For you and your skin.
